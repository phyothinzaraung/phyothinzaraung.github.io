BurmoBot - Android
Burmobot is an intelligent and interactive mobile application designed to assist users in translating words and phrases from English to Myanmar. The app serves as a conversation assistant that remembers recent interactions, suggests translations, and allows users to engage in meaningful conversations with a chatbot interface.
Source Code
Features
  • Word Translation: Translate English words or phrases to Myanmar instantly. The app uses a chatbot-like interface to display the translations.
  • Recent Entries: Never lose track of your translated words. The app keeps a history of your recent translations, allowing you to refer to them anytime.
  • Favorites: Save your most frequently used translations for quick access.
  • Text-to-Speech: Listen to the pronunciation of any translated word or phrase with built-in text-to-speech functionality.
  • Smooth and Intuitive UI: The clean, user-friendly design makes it easy to start translating immediately.
  • Interactive Chat Interface: Engage in a real-time chatbot experience that translates your input and gives you feedback immediately.
Technologies Used
  • Android Development: Built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose for a modern, efficient UI.
  • Clean Architecture: The app follows the Clean architecture for a clean separation of concerns and easy maintainability.
  • Room Database: Powered by Room for local storage of recent and favorite entries, allowing persistent data management.
  • Hilt Dependency Injection: Dependency injection is handled using Hilt to manage app dependencies in a simple and scalable way.
  • Text-to-Speech (TTS): Integrated Text-to-Speech feature for word pronunciation.
  • StateFlow: Uses StateFlow for handling UI-related data in a lifecycle-aware way.
